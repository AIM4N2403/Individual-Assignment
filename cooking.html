Sambal Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 25-30 large red chili peppers or 500g
  • 60 bird's eye chili peppers or 300g
  • 4-5 shallots
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 1 bulb or 12 cloves garlic
  • 3 inches shrimp paste (belacan)

Preparation Method:

  1. Heat oil and fry the shallots and garlic until soft. Remove and drain the oil.
  2. Fry the red chilies and bird's eye chilies until they are soft and wilted. Remove and drain. You can fry the chilies together with the shallots.
  3. Fry the tomatoes separately.
  4. Grind all the ingredients in a chopper. If using a blender, add a little oil before grinding.
  5. Heat oil and fry the shrimp paste until crispy.
  6. Add the ground ingredients and sauté until the oil separates.
  7. Add sugar, salt, and seasoning powder. Stir well.
  8. Turn off the heat and transfer to a glass jar.
  9. Cover the glass jar, but not tightly, and let the sambal cool down first before sealing it tightly.

Aglio Olio Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Olive oil
  • Chicken
  • Salt
  • 1 bulb or 12 cloves garlic
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Parsley
  • Salt to taste
  • Parmesan

Preparation Method:

  1. Cook the pasta and reserve the water
  2. After that, gently cook the garlic and put chicken or other protein(Optional)
  3. Season the garlicky oil with parsley, red pepper flakes and other herbs
  4. Incorporate the pasta water
  5. Coat the pasta in the sauce
  6. Put parmesan and ready to serve
